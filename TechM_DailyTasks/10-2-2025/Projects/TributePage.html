Mahesh Babu

Born: August 9, 1975

Profession: Indian Actor, Producer, Philanthropist

About Mahesh Babu

Mahesh Babu is one of the most popular and influential actors in Telugu cinema. Known for his charismatic screen presence and versatile acting skills, he has delivered numerous blockbuster films. Apart from acting, he is actively involved in humanitarian efforts through his charity foundation.

Notable Achievements

  • Winner of multiple Filmfare and Nandi Awards.
  • Known for movies like Pokiri, Srimanthudu, Bharat Ane Nenu, and more.
  • Runs a non-profit organization, "Heal A Child" Foundation.
  • Brand ambassador for several top brands in India.
